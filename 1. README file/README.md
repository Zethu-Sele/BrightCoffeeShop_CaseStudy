# Bright-Coffee-shop-Sales  
# Background 
Brightcoffee  has recently appointed a new CEO whose mission is to grow the company’s revenue and improve product performance. The Business Intelligence team was provided with a dataset titled “Bright Coffee Shop Sales”, which captures daily transactional information from a coffee shop. The team was tasked to extract actionable insights from
historical data and prepare a presentation to assist the CEO in decision-making.

# Project Planning  

Below are tools used to complete the project: 
1. Data Source - Microsoft .csv file from Point of sale systems across all stores.  
2. Project Planning - MIRO & Canva  
3. Data Processing - Databricks  
4. Data Analysis - Microsoft Excel  
5. Data Presentation - Canva  
6. Results were presented to management on 31 March 2026

# Summary 
Brightcoffee Shop consists of 3 stores, namely: 1. Lower Manhattan 2. Astoria 3. Hell's Kitchen  
The data was collected over a period of 6 months (01.01.2023 - 30.06.2023). Data shows that January to March have lower sales, we start seeing a gradual increase in April, and sales go even higher between May and June. This can be attributed to a few factors like seasons, the first three months are hotter, which results in lower quantity demanded for coffee. Naturally, colder months will drive higher demand for sales.  
Individual store performance is similar, the share of revenue is very closely linked for all stores, with a market share of 34% for Hell's Kitchen, Lower Manhattan and Astoria, both at 33%. A conclusion can thus be made that store location is not a huge determining factor for sales, as stores in different locations perform quite similarly.  

# Recommendations  

1. Introduce Seasonal Products  
   Launch Iced beverages (e.g iced coffee, power smoothies, cold brew) during warmer months
   Bundle with bakery items to increase basket value
This will help boost revenue during warmer months.

2. Introduce Targeted promotions
   Run day-specific promotions e.g "Tuesday treat", "Weekend combo"
This will assist with increasing revenue on days where revenue is lower.

3. Focus on product-level performance
   Top 30 products that drive +60 % of total revenue must always be in stock  
   Top 10 products in the top 3 performing categories need less advertising; advertising for these products can be redirected to     different products to drive revenue.

4. Bundle sale by time of day
   Combos for the morning
   Afternoon deals (these can be discounted refills and or snacks for the afternoon rush)

# Key Takeaway
   Brightcoffee's growth opportunity lies in optimising product strategy, customer behaviour, and pricing. Data shows that the growth opportunity does not lie in store expansion.

By leveraging seasonal trends, optimising product mix, and implementing targeted promotions, the business can increase revenue, boost product performance, and stabilise sales throughout the year.
